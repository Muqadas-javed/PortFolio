Public Local Transport Guider 🚏

Public Local Transport Guider which is a Full Stack native mobile application designed to provide route recommendations for traveling using public local transport. The purpose of the software is to assist users, including amateur local transport users and those seeking cost-effective travel options, in finding the best local transport route from their current location to their desired destination. The application utilizes route planning modules and traffic prediction to determine the nearest local transport stop, the local vehicle cost (fare), and provide the estimated time for the local transport vehicle based on the recommended route. It also offers guidance from the user's current location to the nearest local transport bus/wagon stop if they are not in close proximity.
